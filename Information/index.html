How to Sell on AuctionPal

Selling your items on AuctionPal is a straightforward and rewarding process. Follow these simple steps to get started:

  • Create an Account: Sign up for a free AuctionPal account to access our seller features and start listing your items.
  • List Your Items: Use our intutive listing tool to create detailed listings for your items. Include clear photos, accurate descriptions, and any relevant details to attract potential buyers.
  • Set Your Price: Decide on a starting bid or set a fixed price for your items. You can also choose to include a reserve price for added security.
  • Monitor your Auction: Keep Track of your auction's progress and engage with potential buyers. Answer any questions they may have and provide additional information as needed.
  • Complete the Sale: Once your auction ends, work with the winning bidder to arrange payment and shipping. Follow our guidelines to ensure a smooth and secure transaction for both parties.
  • Receive payment: Once the transaction is complete, you'll receive payment for your sold items. Congratulations on a successful sale!

How to Buy on AuctionPal

Ready to start bidding and scoring great deals on AuctionPal? Here's how:

  • Create an Account: Sign up for a free AuctionPal account to start browsing and bidding on items.
  • Find items of Interest: Explore our wide selection of items up for auction.
  • Place your Bid: When you find an item you want, place your bid.
  • Monitor Your Bid: When you find an item you want, place your bid.
  • Complete the Purchase: If you're the winning bidder, follow the instructions to complete your purchase. Review the seller's terms and conditions, arrange payment, and coordinate shipping if necessary.
  • Enjoy Your Purchase: Once your item arrives, enjoy your new purchase! Leave feedback for the seller to help build trust and transparency within the AuctionPal Community
